TOT PRIVATE LESSONS

Ages 3-6
These one-hour lessons are just the right length to help your little one build their confidence on snow in a safe one-on-one environment. A great value for all ability levels.

Ages 7+

WEEKEND & HOLIDAY 4-HOUR TIME SLOTS


Our weekend & holiday 4-hour time slots mean shorter lift lines, guaranteed parking, and no road delays. When your 4-hour time slot expires, visit Guest Services, and if there is availability, reload at no cost for another four hours.


On weekdays, choose your start time and stay as long as you want.
